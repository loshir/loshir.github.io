Followed by relief because you were alright, all things considered, your petit foot still broken.

It opened up the door for me to see that I just cannot live without you. That I absolutely would struggle to survive if you were not here with me.

And I want to protect you, and us, at all cost. We should remain healthy and well taken care of so that we can still take care of each other.
But at the same time it's reassuring because it is the purest, most tangible form of love one can feel. It is the very manifestation of our mind and bodies that they are bound to each other. It is uncontrollable and beautiful. We need each other. We cannot without each other.
