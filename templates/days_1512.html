People sure like to complicate their lives with a grand proposal and wedding but, comme d'hab, we like to do things differently.

People also tend to focus more on the wedding day than the married life, which to me is simply stupidity. How can you care more about one day than about the rest of your life. You should ensure that the person you're with is someone you'd want to hear the voice of every morning. You should ensure that this person will make you horny all the time.

Just another Monday.
I want to ensure that you understand that, with me saying that, I mean to say that, I was so sure, so undoubtful, that it felt natural and normal for me to get married to you.
