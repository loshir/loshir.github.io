Ton rire qui sait surtout guérir mes blessures

Mistral Gagnant - Renault

I've always loved this song a lot. I love how sad it actually is while sounding so sweet. It's the perfect reflection of how life is often very bitter sweet. Today though, it really made me think about all the positive things about you and us. I would like to say that not even time could erase how good you make me feel, so take that Renault. Time can take your mistral gagnant but not my feelings for my petit wife!

I really, really hope you enjoy this one. I've been wanting to sing it since the first petit song but it never sounded right. I think it did today and I hope it sounds good to you too.
