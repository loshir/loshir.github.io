Special Gift

On se retrouvera - Francis Lalanne

I decided to record this little song for you because I've always liked it despite it being quite maccabre.
I like how it expressed that their bond should be unbreakable even after death.
I like to think that nothing could separate us. Not even that sort of fatality.

Please enjoy.
I know it could be better but I didn't want to lack the time to launch the app and prepare the next pages.
Ps : Use your earphones and don't play it too loud for a better experience!
